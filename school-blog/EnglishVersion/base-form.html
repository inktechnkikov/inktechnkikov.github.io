ABOUT US

The system " My virtual school " can be used by both teachers and students. It would be in great use for all levels of education in Bulgaria. After you sign in,you would be able to see the interesting modules, an electronic system for homework and many useful resources.

FOR TEACHERS



Teachers would be able to post homework for student,see and check their work. The should take notes and tell the student's mark at school.

FOR STUDENTS

There are many opportunities for the students as well. They will be able to follow the schedule with their homework, do it on their computers and then quickly send it electronically to their teacher.
